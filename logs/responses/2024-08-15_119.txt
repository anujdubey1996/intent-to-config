Intent: Deploy the registry-job-operator with a single replica in the registry-system namespace.
The job operator deploys several pods for the registry services.
The operator should have a rolling update strategy that allows two unreplicated pods to be unavailable during an update.
In addition, the operator should have resource limits of 800Mi of Memory and 1 core of CPU.

Configuration:

```yaml
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
 metadata:
   name: registry-job-operator
   namespace: registry-system
 spec:
   replicas: 1
   strategy:
     rollingUpdate:
       maxUnavailable: 2
   selector:
     matchLabels:
       app: registry-job-operator
   template:
     metadata:
       labels:
         app: registry-job-operator
     spec:
       serviceAccountName: registry-job-operator-sa
       containers:
       - name: registry-job-operator
         image: registry.example.com/operator:1.0.0
         resources:
           limits:
             memory: 800Mi
             cpu: 1
         ports:
         - containerPort: 8080
```

This configuration creates a deployment named `registry-job-operator` in the `registry-system` namespace. The deployment specifies a rolling update strategy with a maximum of 2 unavailable pods during the update process. The pod has resource limits set to 800Mi of memory and 1 core of CPU. The container image used is `registry.example.com/operator:1.0.0` and it listens on port 8080.
